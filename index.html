Find Your Happy Life Partner

Finding someone special in your life is not easy but there are ways to better your chances.

Step 1 - Accept yourself and others

First: Realize that you are awesome! And others are too

Everything starts with you. You were created unique. Realizing it can be the bravest thing you ever do. It often also is the first step to finding the person you wish to spend the rest of your life with.

Find peace with what you can’t control

Appreciate what you have

Look at the positive side of things

Open your mind to new things



Second: Be Likeable!

The measuring stick is how you treat others. Nobody’s perfect so why should you expect perfection in a partner? Being likeable not only expands your network to meet more people but also trains you to embrace the strengths and weaknesses of your “perfect” match!

Identify other people’s strengths and focus on it

Learn to listen and empathise

Expect nothing but appreciate when you’repleasantly surprised

Resist the urge to talk about others without them present

Step 2 - Enjoy being “single” as a journey

Enjoy the journey with an eye on the destination.

When it comes to finding the love of your life the journey is as important as the destination because quite simply the journey makes the destination! If you can’t enjoy your own company then who will?



Have a sense of humour

If you can laugh at the emotional tragedies of being single then you know you’re in good shape.

Step 3 - Be Proactive

You can’t expect great things to happen without effort.

If things are going to happen then it has to start from you. Let go of whatever is outside your control but only after you make your own destiny with the things you can.

Take every opportunity to meet people and establish what it is you’re looking for in a partner but keep an open mind.
